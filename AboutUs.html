Gabonese cuisine.
18 Kings Avenue, NorthCliff, Randburg, 2140

We are a team who is trying to help people who are experimenting culture shock, precisely from Gabon. Why Gabonese food?
Restaurant G9 is an idea of Miss Naomie Premice Moughola, Gabonese student at Monash South Africa University, who has also been victim to adaptation difficulties of culture shock in SA in 2022. It is a pleasure for her to share a passion of cooking with you. We are doing our best so that Gabonese people feel comfortable in unfamiliar territories. But don't worry, non Gabonese people. It's your home too. Africa wins!

Naomie Premice, Libreville, Gabon.
